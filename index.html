Questions, answered

What is the one-time onboarding fee?

₹4,999 covers everything to go live: data migration, OTA connections, staff training and your dedicated relationship manager's setup time. It is charged once per property, never again. Coupon codes are available on longer billing terms — the checkout shows you the code that applies to your plan, up to a fully-waived onboarding fee on yearly billing.

How does per-room pricing work?

Basic and Advance both include 15 rooms, and you can add as many rooms as you want on either plan. Beyond 15, Basic adds ₹50 per room per month and Advance adds ₹150 — the difference reflects the real cost of syncing each extra room's rates and availability to OTAs and Google. You only ever pay for the rooms you actually operate.

How is a homestay priced?

By the unit, not the key. One listing — however many bedrooms or keys are inside it — is one unit, and Homestay starts at exactly one unit. Each additional listing you operate adds ₹500 per unit per month.

What's in the setup kit?

Printed QR codes — you choose the quantity of each: self check-in QRs, room dining QRs, and table QRs for your restaurant. Then pick one branding level for the whole kit: standard (DigiStay design with your property name, ₹200 per QR), co-branded (your logo alongside DigiStay, ₹300), or fully custom-branded with design service included (₹400). All kit items are one-time purchases delivered at onboarding.

Can I start monthly and switch to yearly later?

Yes — that's the recommended path if you want to try before committing. And if you upgrade to a longer term within your first 90 days, we credit the onboarding-fee difference back to your invoice.

What's the difference between the Basic and Advanced Channel Manager?

The Basic Channel Manager (an add-on on Basic and Homestay) syncs rates and availability directly to 50+ major OTAs — Booking.com, MakeMyTrip-Goibibo, Agoda, Airbnb, Expedia, Trip.com and more — with 400+ integrations in total via Channex, for every room on your plan. The Advanced Channel Manager, built into the Advance plan, connects our advanced distribution network of 2,000+ channels and partners worldwide — OTAs, GDS, wholesalers and metasearch — plus Google Hotel ARI and the revenue-management tool. Tap "view integrations" anywhere on this page to browse both directories with logos.

Are prices inclusive of GST?

Prices are exclusive of GST. 18% GST is added at checkout and shown clearly before you pay — you'll receive a GST invoice for input credit.
