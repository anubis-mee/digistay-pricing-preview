Common questions

What is the one-time onboarding fee?

It covers everything to go live: data migration, OTA connections, staff training and your dedicated relationship manager's setup time — ₹1,999 for Homestay, ₹2,999 for Basic and ₹4,999 for Advance, all taxes included. It is charged once per property, never again. Coupon codes are available on longer billing terms: 25% off on quarterly, 50% off on half-yearly, and fully waived on yearly — checkout shows you the code that applies.

How does per-room pricing work?

Basic and Advance both include 15 rooms, and you can add as many rooms as you want on either plan. Beyond 15, Basic adds ₹50 per room per month and Advance adds ₹150 — the difference reflects the real cost of syncing each extra room's rates and availability to OTAs and Google.

How is a homestay priced?

By the unit, not the key. One listing — however many bedrooms or keys are inside it — is one unit, and Homestay starts at exactly one unit. Each additional listing adds ₹500 per unit per month.

What's the difference between the Basic and Advanced Channel Manager?

The Basic Channel Manager (an add-on on Basic and Homestay) syncs rates and availability directly to 50+ major OTAs — Booking.com, MakeMyTrip-Goibibo, Agoda, Airbnb, Expedia and more — with 400+ integrations in total via Channex. The Advanced Channel Manager, built into the Advance plan, connects our advanced distribution network of 2,000+ channels and partners — OTAs, GDS, wholesalers and metasearch — plus Google Hotel ARI and the revenue-management tool.

Are prices inclusive of GST?

Yes — every price on this page already includes 18% GST. Nothing is added at checkout, and your renewal charge is exactly the number you see. Your invoice shows the full GST break-up, so if your property is on the 18% slab (rooms above ₹7,500) you can still claim input credit.
